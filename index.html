The Story

Located in Crisfield, Maryland this small town DJ has a lot of sound. Playing at parties and bars on the Eastern Shore of Maryland, DJ Specx has quickly become a hit amongst locals. What started out as a hobby evolved into a job for this young DJ. DJ Specx spends hours every day updating his music library and practicing new mixing techniques.

INFO

Info

Using top of the line Bose Speakers and a Professional mixer, DJ Specx can make any party better. Specx prefers working with more modern music like, pop, modern rock, dubstep, techno, electronica, and much more. Specx also has a large library of classic rock, alternative, and country.
