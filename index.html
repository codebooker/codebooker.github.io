About DJ Specx

The Story

Located in Crisfield, Maryland this small DJ'ing organization has a lot of sound. Playing at parties and bars on the Eastern Shore of Maryland, DJ Specx has slowly become a hit among locals. What started out as a hobby evolved into a full time job for this young DJ. Specx spends hours every day updating his music library and practicing new mixing techniques.

What I Do

Info

Using top of the line Bose Speakers and a Professional mixer, DJ Specx can make any party better. Specx prefers working with more modern music like, pop, modern rock, dubstep, techno, electronica, and much more. Specx also has a large library of classic rock, alternative, and country.
