DJ Specx

The Story

Located in Crisfield, Maryland this small town DJ has professional, big city sound. Playing at parties and bars on the Eastern Shore of Maryland, DJ Specx quickly became a hit amongst locals. What started out as a hobby evolved into a career for this young DJ. DJ Specx often spends hours every day updating his music library and practicing new mixing techniques in order to stay up to date with the latest styles.

Info

Using top of the line Bose Speakers and a Professional mixer, DJ Specx can make any party better. Specx prefers working with more modern music genres such as; pop, rock, dubstep, techno, electronica, and much more. Specx also has a large library of classic rock, alternative, and country. If Specx does not have the music you need, he can get it. From sea shanties to trap music, if he doesn't have it, and you want to hear it, he will get it.
