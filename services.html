Live Sound Production

We cater to small and medium groups of live musicians. Whether you are a single person who needs a professional soundman, or thirteen musicians who need someone to wrangle the soundboard, DJ Specx is your man



Top Of The Line Equipment

We use 2 Bose L1 Model 2 Speakers with 4 B2 Bass Modules (total of 8, 10" subwhoofers). We have a selection of Heil microphones, including PR35's, PR20's, and a Fin. For a complete list of our equipment, email jack@djspecx.com
